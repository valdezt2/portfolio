Why Work With Jacqueline Valdez

Clinical Education Specialist & Web Designer

Clear communication, structured project management, and accessible, responsive design for your practice or organization.

Slide 1 — Focused on Your Goals

I design web experiences that support your real‑world objectives: attracting clients, clarifying services, and reducing friction for busy clinicians and patients.

Every project begins with a short discovery session to define success metrics, timelines, and constraints so we build exactly what you need—nothing extra, nothing vague.

Slide 2 — Structured, Transparent Workflow

Your project is managed in clear phases: discovery, wireframes, visual design, build, and launch. You always know what is happening and what comes next.

I provide concise check‑in summaries, track decisions, and document changes so your team can review progress quickly without wading through technical jargon.

Slide 3 — Modern, Accessible Front‑End

I use modern HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—plus frameworks like Bootstrap when appropriate— to build responsive layouts that perform well on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Semantic markup, ARIA attributes, and clean CSS help ensure your site is accessible, maintainable, and ready for future updates without a full rebuild.

Slide 4 — Collaborative, Low‑Stress Experience

I translate complex requirements into simple options, present trade‑offs clearly, and recommend a path that respects your budget and time.

You get prototypes to click through, concise feedback forms, and realistic launch plans so the process feels organized—not overwhelming.

Slide 5 — Clinical Education & O&P Expertise

With experience in Orthotics & Prosthetics and clinical education, I understand regulatory language, clinical workflows, and the importance of accurate, patient‑friendly content.

This domain knowledge helps us build sites that speak to clinicians, referral sources, and patients in a way that is both professional and approachable.
