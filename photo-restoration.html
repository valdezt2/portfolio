Digital Photo Restoration

This project showcases the process of restoring a vintage photograph using Adobe Photoshop. The goal was to remove age-related damage, correct exposure, and enhance clarity while preserving the original historical character of the image.

Issues: Scratches, dust, and faded contrast.
