Font Types and Their Role in Design

A quick guide to common font categories and when to use them.

Serif Fonts

Definition: Serif fonts include small finishing strokes at the ends of letters.

Ideal Use: Print materials, editorial content, and formal branding.

Sans-Serif Fonts

Definition: Sans-serif fonts remove the finishing strokes for a cleaner look.

Ideal Use: Digital interfaces, dashboards, and minimalist branding.

Script Fonts

Definition: Script fonts resemble handwriting or calligraphy.

Ideal Use: Decorative headlines, invitations, and expressive accents.

Display Fonts

Definition: Decorative fonts designed to stand out visually.

Ideal Use: Logos, posters, and short attention-grabbing headings.
