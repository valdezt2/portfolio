Font Types and Their Role in Design

Understanding Typography Categories

How font families influence clarity, tone, and visual identity.

Typography shapes how audiences interpret information â€” from clinical education to branding and digital interfaces.

Selecting the right font enhances clarity, accessibility, and emotional tone. This guide explores the five major font categories, their characteristics, and where they are most effective.

1. Serif Fonts

Definition: Serif fonts include small decorative strokes at the ends of letters.

Ideal Use: Print materials, formal documents, and traditional branding.
